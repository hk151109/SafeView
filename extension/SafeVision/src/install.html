Welcome to SafeView!

Thank you for choosing to install the SafeView extension. We are dedicated to providing a safe and secure online experience by automatically filtering inappropriate content across social media platforms and websites.

What is SafeView?

SafeView is an advanced content moderation tool designed to detect and blur suggestive or NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content on the web. Our powerful deep learning models analyze images, videos, and other media to ensure you are browsing safely.

Why Choose SafeView?

  • ✔️ **Real-time Filtering**: Automatically detects and censors NSFW content as you browse.
  • ✔️ **User-Controlled Settings**: Customize your filtering preferences, including the intensity of blur effects.
  • ✔️ **Privacy-Focused**: No personal data is sent to external servers, ensuring complete privacy.
  • ✔️ **Seamless Integration**: Easily integrates with your browser without affecting performance.

Getting Started

Once installed, SafeView will automatically start moderating content as you browse social media and websites. You can adjust your settings through the SafeView icon in your browser toolbar.

We recommend you explore the settings to customize the level of content filtering that best suits your needs. Stay safe online with SafeView!
